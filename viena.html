Iron Invasion

Me and my team were commissioned to make a story drive mobile game with an Kalevalan theme. Vienan Portti OY needed a game for their marketing campaign, and we were happy to step in.

The development and publishing process taught me a lot about mobile development and working with android devices and the Play Store. The project was also a good lesson in working with a bigger team, as well as working with a client.
