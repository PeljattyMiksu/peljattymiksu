Kamkball

After winning a game design competition with my team, Kajaani Univeristy of Applied Sciences commissioned us to make a simple browser game for their marketing campaign. I worked in the team as a full stack developer programming both the game and the online leaderboards.

The project taught me a lot about setting up a server and database, managing tasks with an external development team and working with a client.
